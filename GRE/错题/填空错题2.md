**[错题4.1 双选]** The \_\_\_\_\_ of Maria Irene Fornes’ play Mud --- a realistic room perched on a dirt pile --- challenges conventional interpretations of stage scenery.

A. appeal
B. plot
C. trial
D. setting
E. locale
F. rehearsal

<font color = #6397B3>**逻辑判断**: challenge 表示前后对比, 反义; 反义体现在a realistic room perched on a dirt pile 和  conventional interpretations上, 对空格部分没影响<br>**语义判断**: 和空格部分相对应的就是stage scenery, 所以这里填入场景相关的词, setting/ locale <br>**错误原因**: plot 「阴谋; 计划; 情节」当作「安排」来理解, ==对语义判断有错误==; locale不认识;
</font>

**[错题4.2 双空]** Ironically, an affluent society that purchases much more food than it actually needs suffers because of that \_\_\_\_\_, since in conditions of affluence diseases related to overeating and poor nutrition seem to \_\_\_\_\_.

Blank(i)

A. overabundance
B. practicality
C. commonness

Blank(ii)

D. adapt
E. vex
F. thrive

<font color = #6397B3> **逻辑判断**: ironically表示反义, 体现在affluent society和suffer之间, 对空格语义判断没有影响. <br>**第一空**: because of 表示因果关系, 前后同义重复, "that \_\_\_\_" 指代前面出现过的东西, 那么这里就只能选表示「富裕」的词, overabundance「过剩」<br>**第二空**: since表示后面的话和前面的句子是因果关系. 因为affluent society suffers, 那么可以判断, affluence diseases必然是盛行起来, 这里选择thrive「繁荣」<br>**错误原因**: 对于that表示指代前面出现的东西有些疑惑; 第二句话中对affluence disease的定语有些迷惑, 这里其实没有影响的. </font>

**[错题4.3 双选]** Because of the \_\_\_\_\_ effects of the hot springs, tourists suffering from various ailments flocked to the village's thermal pools.

A. succulent

B. restorative

C. therapeutic

D. ambrosial

E. cerebral

F. redolent

<font color = #6397B3>**逻辑判断**: 没有反义, 所以同义<br>**语义判断**: 因为有"\_\_\_\_\_ effects", 所以有毛病的人都聚集过去. 那么对应应该是「治疗效果」, 选restorative/ therapeutic<br>**错误原因:** 不认识restorative </font>

**[错题4.17 双选]** Cito Gaston, one of the least \_\_\_\_\_ baseball managers, surprised reporters by weeping openly after his team won the play-offs.

A. somber

B. demonstrative

C. insufferable

D. uninhibited

E. morose

F. bountiful

<font color = #6397B3>**逻辑判断:** surprised「令人吃惊的」表示前后反义, 而空格前面还有一个least「最不」否定, 那么空格中应该选一个和后面同向的词<br>**语义判断:** 后面说他weeping openly「公开哭泣」那么第一空待选的就有demonstrative/uninhibited 和 morose/ somber两组<br>**辨析:** demonstrative「情感外露的, uninhibited「无法抑制的」而morose/somber表示「忧郁的」但是根据语义这里吐露的是CG这个人公开的哭泣表达的并不是「悲伤的情绪」而是由于获胜带来的情感失控, 说明前两个更好<br>**错误原因:** 不认识inhibited 和 bountiful; 逻辑分析也有些问题;</font>

**[错题4.18 双选]** That critic's writing is so obscure and dense that upon first reading, one finds its \_\_\_\_\_ hard to penetrate.

A. brevity

B. rigidity

C. floridity

D. tawdriness

E. opacity

F. opaqueness

<font color = #6397B3>**逻辑判断:** 没有反义, 前后同义<br>**语义判断:** 空格和前面obscure/dense 「晦涩, 费解的」同义重复, 那么选opacity/opaqueness<br>**错误原因:** 只知道dense有「稠密」而不知道「晦涩的」的含义, 因此认为前面表示的特点是带有辞藻华丽的含义, 所以误选了CD</font>

**[错题4.29 双空]** The controversial tax fueled a sustained \_\_\_\_\_ that could not be \_\_\_\_\_ by the Prime Minister's impassioned speeches.

Blank(i)

A. uprising

B. conflagration

C. interrogation

Blank(ii)

D. challenged

E. fortified

F. quelled

<font color = #6397B3>**逻辑判断:** 没有反义, 有一个否定, 因为是定语从句, 所以后面一空要取反<br>**第一空:** 同义重复, 有争议的税收激起持续的\_\_\_\_\_, 看选项uprising 「动乱, 暴乱」可以; conflagration「大火; 战争」不合适, 这个词一般用作迷惑; interrogation 「询问」<br>**第二空:** 否定取反, 对应前面的动词fueled「激发」选quelled「镇压, 平息」合适<br>**错误原因:** 不认识uprising被conflagration迷惑</font>

**[错题4.31 双空]** The doctor \_\_\_\_\_ so frequently on disease-prevention techniques that his colleagues accused him of \_\_\_\_\_.

Blank(i)

A. vacillated

B. sermonized

C. relied

Blank(ii)

D. steadfastness

E. inconsistency

F. negligence

<font color = #6397B3>**逻辑判断:** 没有反义, 没有否定, 前后同义<br>**语义判断:** 两空都是描述doctor的表现和评价, vacillated「摇摆, 犹豫」inconsistency「前后不一致」合适<br>**错误原因:** 选了sermonized 「说教」steadfastness「忠诚」doctor经常说教...说明他的忠诚; 且不说sermonized没有「推崇」的含义, 后面accused「指责」也表明第二空应该是一个负面词</font>

**[错题4.32 双空]** A judicious biography must be \_\_\_\_\_ representation that depicts both the strengths and the weaknesses of the subject, avoiding the two extremes of \_\_\_\_\_ and indictment.

Blank(i)

A. a polarized

B. a complimentary

C. an equitable

Blank(ii)

D. discernment

E. eulogy

F. censure

<font color = #6397B3>**逻辑判断:** 没有反义, 同义重复<br>**第一空:** 与judicious「慎重的, 明智的」重复, 也可以根据后面的描述来判断, equitable「公正的, 合理的」合适<br>**第二空:** 空格与indictment并列, 根据上下语义判断, 这里填入反义, eulogy「赞美」<br>**错误原因:** 把complimentary「赞美」与complementary「互补的」弄混了</font>
